After the pickup I head over to North Beach to co host a scavenger hunt with “The Professor”!. For some context a couple years ago I came up with the idea to host an event that essentially combines a scavenger hunt with a game of assassins. The idea being that a bunch of teams have riddles to solve that point them to locations in a fixed area while a separate team of assassins tracks down all the playing teams and tries to kill them. The teams that solve riddles have to avoid dying and figure out who the assassins are. Kind of like a game of among us. It was pretty fun and over the years its underwent a bunch of modifications. “The Professor” came aboard after the first one and now after subsequent games we settled on a new set of rules that we think streamline the process.

Now every team is both a player and an assassin. Rather than having a fixed assassin team that goes after every team its more like the game assassin where every team has a specific target they try to assassinate and if they succeed they inherit the target of the team they killed. Also you get points for solving riddles and killing so even if you die you aren’t really out of the game. On top of that you can spend points to buy the location of your target.

In order to solve the riddles you have to actually go to the location in question. For example if the riddle told you to go to Manny’s you couldn’t just say the answer you have to send a picture with your entire team there.
