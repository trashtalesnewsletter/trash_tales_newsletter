Monday

Work is quite intense this week as I need to ship something that is a big blocker for a big project we are working on. I’m more focused than usual at work this week. I try my best to become one with the code and lock in. This extends past normal working hours as I work late into the night to ship stuff. While intense its also kinda fun. I wouldn’t want to work this intensely all the time but there is something satisfying to it in its own right.

Tuesday

Same as Monday. Progress is being made but there is more to do. Slowly but surely getting closer. Fingers crossed.

Wednesday

Today work progresses the same as it does the previous days. In the middle of the work day I get texts from a couple of people congratulating me on my 200th trash pickup! This is a bit surprising to me as one of them is someone who moved out of the Bay last year and the other is someone I know but not super well (ie wouldn’t get this newsletter) and doesn’t keep up well enough to know that it happened. I learn from one of them that Manny (the Civic Joy Fund leader) sent out a weekly email with this in it

This definitely made my day! Also fun fact, I’ve done trash pickup both before heading out to the airport and right after landing at SFO :D. Hopefully in a couple weeks I’ll have another story like that I can add to the list. Stay tuned to the newsletter two weeks from now to find the answer!

I take a break from work and get dinner with “The Enabler”, “The Dentist”, and “The future Ironman”. We go to Bangkok Thai Street food. Its great to catch up with everyone. Naturally I don’t eat anything but I do have enough fodder for a Yelp review. After a long work week this was exactly what I needed to relax a little bit. One interesting tidbit I learn is that the reason the triathlon is swimming -> biking -> running is that the sports are done from most to least dangerous to make sure people do the more dangerous stuff when they have more energy. After dinner I go home and keep working for a few more hours.

Thursday

Today work eases up a bit and we go out to dinner to celebrate a new team member joining this week. We go to a tapas place called Canela Bistro Bar. This place was rather annoying as they wouldn’t let me eat my MIXT salad while I was there. When they first told me I put my salad on the floor and thought ‘ok fine whatever’. Things quickly go from bad to worse when another waiter comes by and tells me I can’t have food on the floor so they take it and say they’ll give it back once I leave. I think this is the first time in history I ever had a salad get coat checked! Begrudgingly I agree to this. Naturally I don't’ have any of the actual tapas there. I wasn’t going to to begin with and its not like I can support a restaurant with such totalitarian food practices. For shame!

After dinner I go to bar night with the lads. For the first time in forever I get to see “The Philosopher”! Back from his travels in Canada and Europe and ready to once again become a contributing member of society. I get regaled with tales of Europe and how some folks can pay their way through it by being a findom. Maybe in another life where I was hotter I could do that but for now I’m stuck using that tech money to pay for the trip.

While at the bar I chat with “The Quant”. I mentioned that last week I took the Ennegram personality test and get type 7. Normally I don’t think too much about personality tests as I think that while fun they aren’t really that accurate. But I do think I have a pretty strong overlap with the type 7 description. He agrees with me and mentions that I tend to do too many things and might be better served by focusing on a few things. He also suggests that I spend my Friday night chilling at home and thinking about what I should be doing rather than impulsively doing a bunch of things. Thats good advice, as to whether I take it stay tuned.

After bar night I go home and work for a couple more hours before going to bed.

Friday

Today work progresses at an OK pace. Its not quite as intense as I feel myself closing in to a degree but I’m not out of the woods yet. Fingers crossed.

After work I … go on a walk with “The Philosopher”. Sorry “The Quant”, I don’t wind up taking your advice after all. Perhaps another time. We wind up walking from his place in SOMA to Ocean Beach. If its any consolation the walk with “The Philosopher” does, as it often does, provide some interesting opportunities for reflection.

One thing he mentions is that he doesn’t think I’m exactly neuro typical. I’ll admit that I may be a bit quirky but I’m not so sure I’d go quite so far as to call myself neuro divergent. He mentions that compared to some of our other mutual friends I don’t fit the description “well adjusted”. I also learn that apparently at a party we were at at a bar “The hombre” asked multiple women if they would date me and they all said no. I like to think thats because I still had the unicorn hat back then which may have been an immediate flag. Since I’ve been progressing through my fashion arc perhaps I wouldn’t get shot down quite so immediately now :D.

He issues me a challenge that I should try to show up to his Thanksgiving with a date. We shall see if this ends up happening. Check back around episode 50 (which will also be close to the one year anniversary) to find out!

I also find out that he, “Granola girlie,” “The Juggler,” and “The Mosher” amongst others are gonna be going on a camping trip over at The Lost Trail. This quite intrigues me as I think going camping like that would make for some great newsletter content and would be an interesting new experience. He doesn’t quite think that I can handle such a long camping trip, having to dig with a shovel to use a restroom, and eating camping food for many days. This in turn triggers my pride which now makes me want to go just to prove that I can do it.

After coming back from the walk I come up with an idea for a new custom hoodie and stickers.

I’m super excited for this design and can’t wait to wear the new hoodie. I also really want to make stickers with it and give them out to people I think it will add some whimsie to SF :D.

Saturday

The day begins with trash pickup as per usual. We have quite the turnout today. Many regulars like “The Politician,” “The notetaker,” and “The grandma” along with many new faces as well. I bring my airpurifier to trash pickup for reasons that I’ll explain later. When I’m inevitably asked about it I try a new strategy where I respond “Do you want the short answer or the long answer?”. I figured most people would say the short answer but I find its about a 50:50 split. As for why …well we’ll get to that.

After trash pickup I wind up going to an alumni picnic over in Dolo for my old company. Its great to catch up with folks and see what people are up to both at the company and outside. Once again I’m asked about the airpurifier

I would have loved to stay longer but I have a BBQ hosted by “Granola Girlie”. A couple days before the BBQ she sends this text blast

And that ladies and germs is why I carried the air purifier the whole day. I’m reasonably certain that part was targeted towards me as I’m the only person she has seen carry one. When I ask her she sloppily tries to gaslight me

Despite her best efforts I’m still convinced its targeted. The BBQ itself is great! I see “The pasta prince”, “The philosopher”, and an old friend from trash pickup I haven’t seen in a while. I also meet many new friends as well. One of the folks there studies meteorology and its cool learning how we can track what kind of sunlight is absorbed by comparing what comes from the sun vs whats reflected into the atmosphere.

After the BBQ I go back to Haight Ashbury to meetup with “The shadow brigade”! This is the one weekend a month shes in town so I gotta strike while the iron is available. Our hangout mainly consists of us getting dinner and walking around. She has many moments that I find too funny not to put in the newsletter. Here are some samples

For dinner she orders some eggplant and cauliflower. I pay for dinner and when she Venmos she says she was tempted to just say eggplant as the Venmo message but didn’t when she thought it could be misconstrued. She should have done it anyway as its funny and my Venmo is private

When she finds out I read manga and watch anime she loudly says “FUCK YOU”RE A WEEB! BUT YOU HID IT SO WELL!”. This really gets me laughing.

Building off my conversation with “The Philosopher” yesterday I ask her whether she thinks I’m normal and one thing she says is “If you weren’t so confident and outgoing you’d probably be bullied for being a nerd. But your confidence and outgoingness more than makes up for it so people like you”. This also gets me laughing.

Best for last here. She mentions that if she saw me back when I still had my unicorn hat she would likely think I was a quirk chungus. Once I look up what that is I laughed quite a bit.

Its always a treat to hang out with “The Shadow Brigade”. We also talk about a bunch of deep life stuff that I won’t be putting in the newsletter but I greatly enjoyed talking about with you :).

Sunday

The day beings with me doing a couple hours of work before leaving the house to finish up some lingering stuff. I then go over to the Panhandle to witness the start of a Midnight Runners event. I would have gone myself if trash pickup wasn’t calling my name. I do see “The Dentist” and “La Tacqueria royalty” and wish them luck as they head off on their 9 mile run going up Twin Peaks. I mentally send them good luck while I comfortably take the bus.

Trash pickup today is great as per usual. We’ve got some of the usual suspects with “The Raja of Rhythm,” “Always causing a Racquet,” “The notetaker,” and others.

After trash pickup I meet up for a quick lunch with “The Professor” and “The Taco”. I hadn’t seen “The Taco” in a bit so it was great to catch up with her. From there “The Professor” and I go to a tea party that one of our other friends is hosting. While we’re waiting for the host to let us in I catch up with “The gamemaster” and some other friends I hadn’t seen in quite a while. Unfortunately I can’t stick around at the tea party because that was just a way to kill time until my real plan which was to meet up with my family. Since I didn’t know when they were coming I decided to go to the tea party for a bit but since the host was still setting up by the time my parents were in the city I don’t get to have any of the tea. Some of my friends were surprised at how abruptly I left. Heres roughly how it went down when the bus came

Catching up with the family was great. We go to Anatolian table where my 30% off comes in clutch. We also go to a great burger place called Hamburger project over in Divisadero. The food here is actually good and I eat which of course means no Yelp review pending.

After hanging out with my family I go to Dolores park. Part of the reason I come here is because I thought “The Raja of Rhthym” might be there since he sometimes goes there before evening Yoga. While I don’t find him I find something quite interesting. It turns out there is a singles event happening. I notice because there is a sign advertising and like a moth to a flame I go over to see what its about. It turns out however that when I get there the event is just wrapping up. It turns out someone I play Spikeball with is friends with one of the organizers of the event.

From what I could tell the event starts with a game where people put name tags on their back corresponding to different historical figures and people had to figure out who they are. Someone was apparently Kim Kardashian, someone was Julius Caesar, and someone was Monica Lewinsky so we have a whole range of notoriety and accomplishments here. The event itself may be over but some of the folks go to Panchitas afterwards and I figure I may as well because the people there seem cool and could be new friends.

While at Panchitas I talk with this one girl who really catches my attention. She is big into community organizing and hosts a cookbook club where she and some other folks cook recipes from a cookbook once a month. On top of that she apparently went to an accelerator to help folks build community building organizations. As someone who loves community this is a huge green flag to me. She mentions that she likes walking, hiking, and runs. On top of that she gives off great vibes and is single. I know I have to strike here. Alright if you all will permit to be honest (oh wait I’m the dictator of the newsletter so its not like I need permission or anything), I am pretty bad at asking girls out when its not via a dating app. But I gotta do this so I do. As she is leaving I get her instagram and resolve to message her later. I send her a message and don’t check my IG until the rest of the newsletter is finished.

Well that point is now so lets see what happened

And .. no response. Well it was pretty quick I suppose. I was hoping there would be an update for this newsletter but I don’t see the point in delaying so … tune in next week?
