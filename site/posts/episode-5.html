Episode 5

Monday

The week starts off not too interesting at work. After work I go to get dinner with some friends which is super fun. Nothing too vivid to talk about here but the dinner vibes were definitely there. If y’all haven’t been to Udon Mugizo in Japan Town I definitely recommend. We then go to Salt and Straw for dessert. Unfortunately I am trying to reduce sugar so I didn’t get anything despite being tempted by the fact that Salt and Straw was running a dairy free special that month with lots of dairy free choices but in the end cooler heads prevailed and the pistachios I had in my pocket tasted quite good as well.

Tuesday

During lunch at work today I found out that The Melt (a popular grilled cheese sandwich chain with some locations here in SF) is apparently VC funded. By Sequoia no less! One of my coworkers was ranting about this today saying that a grilled cheese sandwich place should not be getting 10M in VC funding when franchising a subway is only 500K. He complained quite vividly about how stupid it was for VCs to fund something like this and that he actively doesn’t go to The Melt for that reason.

After work I have improv class. I hadn’t done improv in quite a few weeks and unfortunately it showed. I was not at the top of my game today. Sure I got some laughs but I could definitely feel the rust. My instructor had a particular note for me that I tend to start scenes with conflicts too much forcing my teammates into fighting with me as part of the scene. Looking back I realize I do tend to use that a lot as a crutch which is a real shame considering there are endless options. It reminded me of an exercise I did in my very first improv class where we all had to pretend we liked each other in the scene no matter what we were doing. Definitely something to remember going forward.

Wednesday

After work I end up going going to China town for the night market with some friends. Because Daniel Lurie just got inaugurated as mayor today there was a big nightmarket / party in China town with some famous EDM artist named Zhu.

Before Zhu we went to a restaurant in Chinatown where my friends aunt works which was pretty neat.

After that its Zhu time. In my previous newsletters I mention that I went to Santacon and the special trash pickup which were both super crowded, but those were both just warmups compared to this. It was CROWDED! We tried going to the center in front of the stage and it wasn’t just that I could barely move but that I was struggling to keep a float amidst the sea of strangers. At one point I was seriously worried something like this was going to happen and I grabbed a street sign to try to keep up. Whats worse was that we were on an incline so I was seriously worried that if I tripped it would cause a domino effect. While I do have an actual photo I figured it would be more fun to illustrate this via stick figures. The one screaming is me. We decide that we need to get out of the crowd so we push our way through to get to a spot where its only crowded as opposed to suffocating. This is mostly me just awkwardly shoving my way through while apologizing a lot as this diagram below demonstrates.

Once we get to a more manageable spot it becomes a lot more fun! We’re all having a good time vibing to the beat which goes til pretty much exactly 9. Its a bit surprising that it ended so early but it was fun. Afterwards we go get boba at plentea and hang out there for a while.

Thursday

At work today I learn from some of my coworkers with young kids that the process for applying to preschools is super competitive! Apparently some of them have the parents fill out these big questionnaires with lots of questions about the kids and even do playdates as part of the interview process. Yes I wrote that write there is an interview process for a freakin preschool. Jeez what has the world come to. I also learn that there are feeder preschools. Yes I said that right, FEEDER PRESCHOOLS. I mean come on people really?! I dread what things will be like when its time to have the Humzlings go to preschool.

After work I go to play Dune Imperium with friends. Ever since last week I had shinsplints because I ran too much and I learned the hard way that even sprinting 200m was enough to have my shins writhing in pain for an hour. This put an unfortunate damper in my plans to go running with some friends later in the week. We put a rule that whoever is late has to play the worst character in this game and since that was me thats what I do but as always Dune Imperium is a fun time.

Friday

Its my friends birthday party today!

One of my favorite parts of birthdays is giving gifts to people. To be honest I prefer giving gifts than receiving because its a fun way to see what kind of thing I can get for a friend based on what they like and any past memories. For this birthday I got my friend a bird plant globe because shes into both plants and birds. My personal favorite gifts to get for people are the ones that combine their interests with any specific memories based on my interactions. An example of this would be when I got one of my friends whose super into postcards a custom postcard with pictures of us from the last year, and when I got another friend a custom made clock with him on it saying “I’ll forgive you in time” (it makes sense in context).

While this gift isn’t quite my best work I’m still reasonably satisfied with it. Along those lines I also like custom making birthday cards as well. And this is the card I custom made

Front Inside

An unexpected silver lining of my present was the fact that I brought in a Whole Foods bag because I didn’t have anything nice to bring it but the bag was useful for helping to carry other people's presents to make packing easier.

I picked this card design because my friend is a dentist hence the tooth and Ahh-pun, and as mentioned above she likes plants and birds. I’d say the card was pretty good, not perfect but pretty good.

For this party we first go to Freekah in the Mission to get dinner and it was quite solid. The Kofta kabobs and hummus were great. We then bring out the cake that we got for her! As mentioned shes a dentist so we got her a cake that said “9 out of 10 dentists recommend celebrating your birthday”. It was a tough decision in the group chat deciding which joke to use and while I can’t claim credit for the winning joke it was indeed a great winning joke and most importantly she seemed to think so as well!

The cake was supposed to be a surprise but due to one Humza’s shortcomings the plan ended up getting leaked (probably because said Humza asked what kind of cake she’d like), it was known well in advance. Ah well.

Happy birthday Alyson! Hard to believe we became such good friends in only a few weeks and that we’ve clicked so well!

After dinner we go to a Emo vs Pop Punk music video party where the DJ plays Emo / Punk rock music videos. Never been to anything like this so definitely curious to see what its like!

Between dinner and this party there is however a stupid Humza moment that needs to be recorded. As we are getting ready to go from one venue to the other some people need to dip out. One of the folks whose leaving says we should do some headbangs for him, and I proceed to say “I’ll bang one out in your honor”. Yeah, I really did say that. To make matters even better this is also the only real interaction I had with this guy so who knows what he thinks of me :D. I strongly feel that reporting stupid Humza moments is one of the most important parts of this newsletter and a core tenant of its existence!

After clearly making a good impression on the friends of my friend we go to the Emo vs Pop Punk party and it was pretty fun!

To be honest I wasn’t too sure what to expect going to something like this but I did quite like the vibes and energy that everyone had. There was even a mosh pit in the front! If it wasn’t for my aforementioned shins I would have considered participating but didn’t want to risk getting pushed and have some other injury come along. Some of the people there were really going at it and charging at each other kinda like pitbulls. I even saw someone do some shadowboxing.

Usually at these sort of events my energy tends to die down as the night goes on but today I wanted to push myself and gave myself the goal of staying until what I deemed to be a critical mass had left. I’ll admit I did need to take a couple of breaks from the main action but it was still fun the whole way through. The best part was right around midnight when All Star came on. No matter what was happening before there is always more energy for All Star! Eventually it does look like a critical mass of people have gone home and I then do the same.

Saturday

The morning begins with trash pickup as per usual. When we get the trash supplies from the usual place that hosts us we found this little guy inside

Its not that big a deal but was definitely surprising! Unfortunately I don’t think this mouse ever had a name so I posthumously dub him Cooper. This trash pickup is dedicated to Cooper!

While picking up trash I found some most excellent curb candy: This mens style book! To be honest its hard to imagine seeing something like this get made today so I want you all to take a second and appreciate it before moving on.

Have you appreciated it yet? No cheating please.

Ok now that I’ve made sure you appreciated it its time to move on.

Unfortunately I can’t stay as long as I normally do post trash pickup because one of my friends is moving to New York on Sunday and is supposed to be having a going away party shortly afterwards.

I get to the going away party only to find the host saying that he couldn’t come at the last minute. Not gonna lie I was feeling pretty disappointed since I cut other plans short specifically to come to this.

This disappointment lasts for all of 10 seconds when he then pops out behind and surprises me! This was a great troll and I highly appreciate it!! When he popped out he even said that he hopes this little trick will make it into the newsletter and it most certainly did. Leo you really got me and I couldn’t be happier!

We then have Pineapple King and North beach pizza which seems to be quite delicious based on what the folks there were saying. Sadly my time here too was a bit limited because I had a prior commitment before so I couldn’t stick around as long as I like.

Before talking about that I do want to say one more goodbye to Leo! It was really fun getting to know you through both trash pickups and midnight runners and neither will be the same without your vibrant energy, warmhearted happy go lucky personality, and endless sense of adventure.

I’m really happy you’re taking this step closer to your dream and while I know the move to London isn’t confirmed yet I’m confident that will happen soon as well. I know its something you wanted for years now and I can’t wait to hear about all the soon to be adventures that will happen there. I wanted to leave you with this meme I made of all of us wishing you good luck! Go get em King! Leo bonus points if you can guess what manga this is from, we’ve talked about this series before :D.

“I’m deeeeeead” - Leo after reading this meme probably. I can read that quote in your voice :D

After saying goodbye I head to Game Parlor for a date. Before the date I went to get her some flowers. I’ve never actually gotten someone flowers before a first date and thought it would be interesting to try. I give them to her right when the date starts and she said she likes them and their pretty. Of course she could have just been saying that to be nice but she brought them up again at the end and I think thats a good sign (hopefully)!

Overall I thought this date went quite well! She was super nice and fun to talk to and I really felt like we got along well! She also had a super cute smile! Unlike the date I went on last week I do genuinely want to see her again!

One of the nice things about a board game date is that games are a great tool if there is ever a silence. Unlike when I’m with friends silences on a date always worry me a bit so this was a great tool to help keep things moving!

Some of my friends always joke about wanting to stalk me on a date and this time I took them up on it. I told them when and where I was meeting and they came to Game Parlor and saw a bit of the date. They didn’t stick around for very long (like maybe 2 min max of the 2 hour date), and were indistinguishable from other folks there but now I can say I had friends follow me on a date. That bucket list item is now checked off !

After the date I catch up with some of those friends afterwards and we debrief a bit and then hangout and play some boardgames.

I’m actually writing this out of order because I wanted to capture this part while it was still raw. Normally I write the whole newsletter on Sunday but I wanted to capture all my feelings on this while it was happening.

After playing boardgames with friends I learned of a retrospective case of some of my friends hanging out without me which caused me to feel an intense bout of FOMO and insecurity. The whole point of this newsletter is to share the things that *I* find the most interesting and since FOMO is admittedly a big problem I myself deal with I’m choosing to write about it here. I don’t really want to give too many particular details because one of my rules of this newsletter is I try to minimize talking about others in a negative way or any sort of interpersonal relationship problem as much as possible. As a result I know the way I wrote it sounds kinda lame which is fair but adding more details is a hard no for me. I’m bringing this up more so to talk about how I deal with FOMO rather than the situation itself whether my feelings are valid etc nor am I writing this looking for advice about that.

I take the MUNI home and I process said feelings. As its happening my rational and irrational sides duke it out (for any people who watched the movie The Emperor’s new Groove or know some memes from it imagine the angel and devil sitting on Kronk’s shoulders if that makes it funnier). Rationally I know its not really valid, of course my friends like me etc. My irrational side is worried that maybe my friends dpn’t like me as much as I thought etc. One of my personal goals is to get better at dealing with FOMO and not being bothered as much. To be honest I’m not sure the best way to go about it. Perhaps being more mindful and in the moment. Perhaps to be grateful. Perhaps I need to just stop being dumb. In any case its an ongoing journey I suppose.

While taking the MUNI home I notice a girl sitting not too far from me with gauze around her hand and bloodshot eyes. I don’t know exactly what happened but I really wanted to go up to her and say that I hope she’s ok. In the end I decide not to do this because I was worried I might make her feel uncomfortable given shes in a public place and since I don’t know her headspace I might end up making it worse. I’m not sure whether I made the right call, it really was a situation where I wished I could just send some positive vibes. Oh well.

Sunday

For once I would have skipped trash pickup to go on a birthday run with my friends but sadly the ol shin splints vetoed the decision. Because of that I do indeed do trash pickup as per usual. We ran out of grabbers today so I raw dogged picking up trash on Mission street. While I didn’t get to move my legs as much I’m pretty sure I got to exercise a different muscle even more!

While picking up trash I almost pick up someones stuff by accident. The guy was standing kinda far from it which is why I didn’t realize it but he then comes up and says “Learn how to do your job!”. Normally I think this is the sort of thing people would generally feel upset about if they heard someone say it but I felt happy because it gave me an excuse to troll the guy a bit. I grinned and said “Yeah I’m getting better every day! Please forgive me kind sir!”. And he said “Get to it servant.”. The whole thing made me smile which perhaps wasn’t normal but it was my reaction.

I do join the running friends from above later for brunch at Flower + Water pizzeria. If you haven’t been the olives there are quite good and I definitely recommend. They also have some other things like pizza I think but I didn’t really eat as much of that so hard to say how good that is. I think its good from what my friends said anyway. After a little walk I come home to type up the news letter!
